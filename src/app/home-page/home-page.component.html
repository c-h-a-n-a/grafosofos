GrafoSofos

​GrafoSofos is a combination of two Greek words. “Grafo” means “Write” and “Sofos” means “Wise”. This website is an innovative online platform designed to provide budding journalists and writers with extensive materials and interactive lessons on campus journalism. It provides a multifaceted learning experience by combining text and short quizzes, enabling users with the necessary skills and knowledge to flourish in journalistic​ writing within the context of campus reporting.
